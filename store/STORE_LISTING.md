## Full description (both stores)

```
Park2Me turns everyday drivers into each other's best parking source.

When someone is about to leave a parking space, they share it. When you're
looking, you see fresh spots opening around you on a live map — and tap one to
get in-app GPS straight to it.

WHY PARK2ME
• Live spots — see parking opening near you in real time, no refresh needed.
• Verified open — spots a driver has confirmed are open are clearly marked.
• Freshness you can trust — every spot shows how recently it was shared.
• Give parking forward — share your space in a few taps as you leave.
• In-app navigation — turn-by-turn direction and distance to your chosen spot.
• Anonymous by design — no account, no name, no license plate.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Open the map to see fresh spots near you.
2. Pick one and start in-app GPS.
3. Leaving your own spot? Share it so another driver can take it.

Park2Me shows live estimates from the community — spots are not guaranteed, and
you should always park legally and safely. Never interact with the app while
driving.
```

## Apple "App Privacy" cheat sheet

Data collected and **linked to identity? NO** for all (the app is anonymous):

- **Location → Precise Location** — Purpose: *App Functionality*. Not used for
  tracking. Not linked to identity.
- **Identifiers → Device ID (the app's own random anonymous ID)** — Purpose:
  *App Functionality* (anti-abuse / grouping your own spot). Not linked to
  identity. Not used for tracking.

No contact info, no financial info, no health, no browsing history, no
advertising data. **App does not use tracking (ATT not required).**

## Google Play "Data safety" cheat sheet

- **Data collected:**
  - *Location → Approximate & Precise location* — Collected: Yes. Shared: No.
    Purpose: *App functionality*. Processed ephemerally where possible; spots
    auto-delete.
  - *App activity / other (anonymous device ID)* — Collected: Yes. Shared: No.
    Purpose: *App functionality, Fraud prevention*.
- **Data sharing:** None sold or shared with third parties for their own use.
- **Encryption in transit:** Yes (HTTPS).
- **Data deletion:** Data expires automatically; user can stop sharing and
  uninstall. Provide the support email for requests.
- **Location permission:** Foreground ("while in use") only. **No background
  location.**
