# Park2Me — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 9 July 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Park2Me mobile application
("Park2Me", "the app", "we", "us") processes and why. Park2Me is designed to be
**anonymous**: it does not require an account, name, email, phone number, or
vehicle license plate to work.

## 2. Information we process

Park2Me collects the minimum needed to show and share nearby parking:

- **Precise location** — used only while the app is open (foreground). We use it
  to center the map, show nearby spots, verify the pin when you share your
  parking, and provide in-app navigation to a spot. We do **not** access your
  location in the background.
- **Anonymous device identifier** — a random ID generated on your device the
  first time you open the app and stored locally. It lets the backend group your
  own shared spot and apply anti-spam rate limits. It is not linked to your
  identity and is not a hardware/advertising ID.
- **Shared-spot data** — when you share a parking space, we store its
  coordinates, your chosen departure window, and timestamps so other drivers can
  find it. No personal or vehicle-identifying details are collected; car details
  are always stored as a generic, private placeholder.
- **Navigation feedback** — if you tell the app whether you parked, found the
  spot, or could not find it, we store that outcome (and an optional distance) to
  improve spot freshness.

We do **not** collect your name, email, phone number, contacts, photos, license
plate, or advertising identifiers, and we do **not** use third-party analytics or
advertising SDKs.

## 3. How we use the information

- To display live nearby parking and navigate you to a chosen spot.
- To match drivers sharing a spot with drivers looking for one.
- To prevent spam and abuse (rate limiting).
- To keep listings fresh by expiring and removing old or unconfirmed spots.

We do **not** sell your data and do **not** use it for advertising or profiling.

## 4. Data retention

Park2Me is built to hold data only as long as it is useful:

- Shared parking spots automatically expire within minutes of the stated
  departure window and are deleted by an automated cleanup job.
- Anonymous device records are deleted after roughly 24 hours of inactivity.
- Navigation feedback is retained in aggregate to improve reliability.

## 5. Where data is processed (sub-processors)

- **Convex** — provides the backend database and serverless functions that store
  spots and device records.
- **Apple Maps (iOS)** and **Google Maps (Android)** — render the map. Your use
  of the map is also subject to Apple's and Google's respective privacy policies.

## 6. Location permissions

Park2Me requests **"while in use"** location permission only. You can revoke it
at any time in your device settings; the app will then be unable to show nearby
spots or navigate you.

## 7. Children

Park2Me is not directed to children and should not be used by anyone below the
minimum driving age in their jurisdiction.

## 8. Your rights

Depending on where you live (e.g. under GDPR or CCPA), you may have rights to
access, correct, or delete personal data. Because Park2Me is anonymous and holds
no identifying information, the practical way to remove your data is to stop
sharing spots and uninstall the app; unconfirmed and expired spots are deleted
automatically. For any request, contact **[support email]**.

## 9. Security

Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS) and stored with our
backend provider. No system is perfectly secure, but we minimize risk by
collecting as little as possible and deleting it quickly.
